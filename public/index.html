TWENTYCHORE/7

The modern chore manager.


If you are looking for easier way to keep track of your child(ren)'s chore's then look no further!

The first step is creating an account for yourself. Once you login with your account, simply click on "Add Child" to create an account your child will use to login, see what chores they need to complete, and sign off on the chore once they have completed it. Simply repeat the process for each child you have. Your child(ren)'s account(s) will be listed on your home page. Click on the child's name to see a list of the chores you have assigned them, as well as the option to add additional chores. When you click on a chore in the child's list, it will show which days it is to completed. When your child signs off that they completed a chore two buttons will be present next to the day allowing you to sing off that is was actually completed, which resets it on the child's account and adds the point value to their points bank. Or you can click "Redo Chore" if the chore was not actually completed which resets it on the child's account without giving them the points.
